<h1>Getting Started</h1>
<p>Welcome to the OneSchoolBus Driver App! To get started, simply visit
<a href="https://go.gov.sg/osb-driver" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">this link</a>on your smartphone to access the attendance application.
If you're a Chinese speaker, there's a Chinese version available at <a href="https://go.gov.sg/osb-driver-cn" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">this link</a>.</p>
<p><strong>Important Note for Bus Operators:</strong> Please provide these
instructions to your drivers and attendants to ensure a smooth onboarding
process.</p>
<hr>
<h3>Logging In</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Driver/Getting Started/OSB_Drv_01.png">
</div>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Enter Your Phone Number and tap the Login button.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Receive and Enter OTP. You should receive a six-digit One-Time Pin (OTP)
via SMS within the next few minutes. Enter this OTP and tap Next. If you
don’t receive an SMS after 3 minutes, press Back and retry.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Note:</strong> If you encounter an “Invalid phone number” error,
kindly confirm with your bus operator that your phone number is correctly
registered in the system.</p>
<hr>
<h3>Homepage Overview</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Driver/Getting Started/OSB_Drv_02.png">
</div>
<p>You will see your routes listed and their starting times. You can also
find details such as the bus number, start time, and route days.</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Morning routes are in orange. Tap on the route to bring you to the morning
attendance page for the day.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Afternoon routes will be in blue. Tap on the route to bring you to the
afternoon attendance page for the day.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Note:</strong> These routes are assigned to you by your operator.
If you don’t see any routes on this page, please contact your bus operator
to ensure they’ve assigned routes to you.</p>

<h1>Managing Bus Operators</h1>
<p>On this page, you will learn how to view the bus operators serving your
school within One School Bus.</p>
<hr>
<h3>Bus Operator Page</h3>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/School/Navigating OneSchoolBus/OSB_Staff_7.png">
</div>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Click on "Bus Operators" to access the manage bus operator page.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click on "View bus coordinators" to expand and show the details of the
bus coordinators serving your school. Here it will show the name of the
bus coordinator as well as the contact number.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Note:</strong> Bus coordinators can access OSB depending on whether
they have been registered to use OSB. Should their name not appear in this
list, please ask him/her to contact the other existing bus coordinators
to provide them access.</p>

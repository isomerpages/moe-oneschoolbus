<h1>Navigating One School Bus</h1>
<p>On this page, you will learn how to navigate the pages within One School
Bus.</p>
<hr>
<h3>Top Navigation Bar Pages</h3>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/School/Navigating OneSchoolBus/OSB_Staff_3.png">
</div>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Students (Most fields View-only):</strong> Lists your students
who take the school bus, as submitted by the operators. You may input the
FAS status here.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Routes (View-only):</strong> Shows your bus operators' pick-up
and drop-off routes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Attendance:</strong> Allows you to download school bus attendance
reports.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Bus Operators:</strong> View the bus operators serving your school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Help:</strong> Provides access to our FAQ guide and contact information
for support via email.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Data Exercise:</strong> This is the submission tab for the annual
Q1 MOE HQ School Bus Exercise. It involves inputting FAS information and
verifying your operator's data for accuracy. It will turn green when the
exercise is ongoing.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Sign Out:</strong> Logs you out of the OSB portal.</p>
</li>
</ol>

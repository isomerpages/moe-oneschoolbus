<h1>Managing Bus Routes on OSB</h1>
<p>On this page, you will learn how to manage the bus routes you have uploaded
in OneSchoolBus.</p>
<hr>
<h3>Accessing the Bus Routes You Have Uploaded</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Operator/Managing bus Routes on OSB/OSB_Ops_26.png">
</div>
<p>On the top navigation menu:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Select Routes, then select Bus Routes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>All the schools that you are serving will be displayed on the left sidebar,
e.g., ESTL Primary School. Click on the school to see all the routes you
have created for the school, then select one of the routes to view it.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Note</strong>: If you have not created any bus routes, you will
see the image to guide you to upload data. Head on to the page on Creating
Bus Routes (Manual entry) or Upload Bus Routes - (Bulk Update).</p>
<p><strong>Note</strong>: Routes are divided into year tabs to store data
for multiple years. By default, the latest year is selected. To view past
yearsâ€™ data, select the desired year, e.g., 2023.</p>
<hr>
<h2>Delete Bus Route</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Operator/Managing bus Routes on OSB/OSB_Ops_27.png">
</div>
<p>On the top navigation menu, select Routes, then select Bus Routes to display
Routes Summary page.</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>On the sidebar, select the desired route(s) under the school name.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click on the Delete Routes button at the bottom-right of the page.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>A pop-up will appear to confirm. Click on the Delete button on the pop-up
to confirm, or Cancel.</p>
<hr>
<h2>Export Bus Route</h2>
<p>On the top navigation menu, select Bus Routes. Check that the correct
year is selected on the tabs on the left navigation menu. Select the route
that you would like to export.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Operator/Managing bus Routes on OSB/OSB_Ops_28.png">
</div>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Click on Export and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select either:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Export this route, and all the details of this selected bus route will
be downloaded to an excel file.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Export all routes (2023), and all bus routes for the school will be automatically
downloaded to an excel file.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>

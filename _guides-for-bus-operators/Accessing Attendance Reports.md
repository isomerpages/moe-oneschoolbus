<h1>Accessing Attendance Reports</h1>
<p>Learn how to download attendance reports with ease.</p>
<h3>Accessing and Downloading Attendance Reports</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Operator/Managing bus Routes on OSB/OSB_Ops_39.png">
</div>
<p>From the top navigation menu,</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Select Students, then Attendance.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select the date range you wish to view the attendance. Default set to
today.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select the school you wish to download.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select All for a full attendance report, or select a specific bus route
to download the attendance report.</p>
</li>
</ol>

<h1>Managing Students on OSB</h1>
<p>Learn how to view, add, edit, or delete student records. You can also
change a student’s name; details are in “How do I add, edit, or delete
students?”</p>
<hr>
<h2>Accessing My List of Students</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Operator/Managing bus Routes on OSB/OSB_Ops_28a.png">
</div>
<p>On the top navigation menu,</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>select Students.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The menu will expand to show all the schools you are serving in MOE. Click
on the school which you want to view.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Operator/Managing bus Routes on OSB/OSB_Ops_29.png">
</div>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The Name, Gender, Class, and Bus Fare columns can be sorted in ascending
or descending order.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are divided into year tabs to store data for multiple years.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To find a specific student, type part or all of their name into the search
bar. The list of students gets filtered as you type.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<hr>
<h2>Add New Student Details</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Operator/Managing bus Routes on OSB/OSB_Ops_30.png">
</div>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Click on Add Student beside the search bar.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>An Add student pop-up will appear. Fill in the fields accordingly.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click on Save to save your changes.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<hr>
<h2>Edit Existing Student Details</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Operator/Managing bus Routes on OSB/OSB_Ops_31.png">
</div>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Tap on any of the fields.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The field will expand, and you will be able to type in the changes and
edit.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click on Save Changes to save your changes; otherwise, no changes will
be saved.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Note:</strong> NRIC / FIN (last 4 characters) is not a required
field. The purpose is for internal identification when there are students
with identical names, and OSB is unable to match the student name accurately
in the MOE system.</p>
<hr>
<h2>Delete Existing Students</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Operator/Managing bus Routes on OSB/OSB_Ops_32.png">
</div>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>In the student table, find the students you want to delete.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Next to the student name, under #, select the checkbox to select the student.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The total number of students selected will be shown at the bottom left
of the screen.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click the Remove rows button at the bottom right-hand corner of the page.
A pop-up will appear to confirm. Click on the Remove rows button again
to confirm, or Cancel.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Note:</strong> If you remove a student from this list, the student
will be automatically removed from all bus routes as well.</p>
